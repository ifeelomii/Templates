"An Evening with Mozart" - Chamber Music Concert

Experience the magic of Mozart in an intimate chamber music concert featuring some of the world's most talented musicians. Led by renowned pianist and conductor Maria Rodriguez, this special evening will showcase some of Mozart's most beloved works, including his iconic Piano Sonata No. 11 in A Major and the playful Divertimento in D Major. Hosted at the beautiful and historic venue of the City Theatre, the concert promises to be an evening of elegance and sophistication. The intimate setting of the chamber music concert will allow you to fully immerse yourself in the beauty of the music, with each note and melody brought to life by the skill and passion of the performers. Don't miss this rare opportunity to experience the timeless beauty of Mozart's music in an intimate and unforgettable setting. Book your tickets now to secure your place at this one-of-a-kind event.
